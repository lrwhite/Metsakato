Metsakato - Deforestation

Deforestation is the permanent destruction of forests in order to make the land available for other uses.


What is our purpose?

Our purpose consists of us educating the public as our main goal. We want the public to become aware about the seriousness that deforestation has on our planet. We also want to show areas where the most deforestation occurs and come up with a solution to solve the problem. We need your help to save our planet!
